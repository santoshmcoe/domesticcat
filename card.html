Domestic Cat

Domestic cats, no matter their breed, are all members of one species.

  • Common Name:Domestic Cat
  • Scientific Name:Felis catus
  • Type: Mammals
  • Diet: Carnivores
  • Size: 28 in
  • Weight: 5 to 20 lbs

Felis catus has had a very long relationship with humans. Ancient Egyptians may have first domesticated cats as early as 4,000 years ago. Plentiful rodents probably drew wild felines to human communities. The cats' skill in killing them may have first earned the affectionate attention of humans.
